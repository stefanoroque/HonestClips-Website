Two Friends Traveling Around the World

Initially, Adam and Stefano were planning on doing completely separate backpacking trips. Adam was supposed to go to South East Asia and Stefano was supposed to go to Europe. When the COVID-19 pandemic grinded all international travel to almost a complete halt, both of their original trips were cancelled. After months of waiting for things to settle down, Adam and Stefano saw that Europe was opening their borders to Canadians. They decided to hop on a plane with the hope that they could blend both of their trips together into one huge trip. The best of both worlds!
